## Profile
Fesothe, known professionally as **Velvian Fesothe**, is a multifaceted indie developer, 3D artist, and founder of **Furtainment** and **SnackFerret Studio**. With a deep passion for virtual worlds and digital creation, he transforms imaginative concepts into vibrant 3D models, animations, and games. A tech enthusiast since the Commodore 64 era, he also champions community initiatives, notably supporting rabbit rescue efforts through his work.
