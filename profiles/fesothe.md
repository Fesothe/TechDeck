## Profile

Fesothe, known professionally as **Velvian Fesothe**, is a multifaceted indie developer, 3D artist, and founder of **Furtainment** and **SnackFerret Studio**. With a deep passion for virtual worlds and digital creation, he transforms imaginative concepts into vibrant 3D models, animations, and games. A tech enthusiast since the Commodore 64 era, he also champions community initiatives, notably supporting rabbit rescue efforts and animal welfare through his work.

## Origin

The name **Fesothe** originated as an early online identity used by Jonathan Curley during the period when he was experimenting with his personal branding, digital presence, and creative identity separation. It first appeared in connection with his Second Life fursona and early web projects, including **Highwire Skys** (highwireskys.com), Second Life served as one of the initial spaces where he tested naming conventions, visual identity, and his fursona structure.

Over time, “Fesothe” became the consistent handle representing Jonathan’s **creative persona**, used across platforms for:

- 3D art  
- game development  
- technical art  
- digital identity work  
- community‑facing creative projects  

It functions as a **public‑facing creative identity persona**, distinct from his legal name, allowing separation between:

- professional work  
- creative output  
- persona‑based branding  
- Furtainment‑related projects  

The name does have a fictional backstory and narrative role that evolves through Jonathan’s digital work and became the stable persona associated with his artistic and technical output.
